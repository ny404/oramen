ORAMEN: Japanese Izakaya Ramen & Sushi

Nestled in the heart of Chelsea, Oramen offers an inclusive variety of Japanese staples, catering to all food preferences. From hearty, soul warming ramen to fresh sushi, Oramen provides a welcoming atmosphere that allows diners to feel at home, along with a taste of Japan.

What truly separates Oramen from the rest is our deep rooted philosophy that our customers deserve only the best ingredients, dishes and experiences. Our customers become family and everyday, we strive to provide consistently reliable dishes and service that keep our customers coming back.
